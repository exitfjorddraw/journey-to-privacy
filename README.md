# journey-to-privacy
Documenting my journey toward better digital privacy

My story:
Until the start of 2026 I was among those that say "I have nothing to hide" when faced with the topic of privacy and surveillance in our world today.
I credit the book Nexus by Yuval Harari as the critical turning point for me changing my view to "Privacy is an important principle now and for our future".
There were also many other conversations and experiences that converged to change my understanding and beliefs too.

Now, that I have started down this path, I want to document the things I learn here publicly and semi-anonymously so that I can both share my findings as well as discuss priorities and best practices with others, some of which undoubtedly have much more experience than I do.

Here is my plan:
1. Record the apps and tools I used before and after starting the journey (and update them as they change)
2. Attempt to transparently provide evidence and confidence scores to show how some apps are improvements in privacy over others
3. Provide details about the process of switching apps including challenges and benefits

Organization of Documents:
1. CurrentApps.md (for part 1 of the above plan)
2. Evidence.md (for part 2 of the above plan)
3. TransitionGuides.md (for part 3 of the above plan)
